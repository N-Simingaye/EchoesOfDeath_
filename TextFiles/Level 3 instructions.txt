Level 3 instructions
Chamber of secrets
is a deadly game cloaked in mystery.
In each room you will find important 
documents you must read.
Click the left button to move to the left.
Click the right button to move to the right.
Click the down button to move backwards.
Click the up button to move forward.
Your task is to navigate each room, gathering
information and answering questions that
follow.
Ensure the avatar is in between the door 
frame and the avatar is faced forward.
The avatar must be closest to the door
