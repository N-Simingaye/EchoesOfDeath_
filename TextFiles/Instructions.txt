Puzzle of Hops
is a deadly game cloaked in mystery.
Within the diary entries lie secrets-
every word a clue, every phrase a 
thread, unraveling your parents' 
hidden lives.
Answer the questions, uncover the 
suspects, reveal the motive.
But tread carefully. 
The shadows are watching.
The truth is buried deep, and only the 
sharpest mind will survive the game.
Three suspects await discovery.
Fail... and the price is fatal.
Dare to play?
